Intermediate for Adults

Learn basic English vocabulary, grammar and pronunciation while working on your speaking, listening, reading and writing skills. Learn about topics like jobs & workplaces, family, numbers, food, technology and more! The classes are fun and use a variety of interactive resources like activities, videos and audio recordings based on National Geographic books. Assessments will be done periodically with useful feedback given to give you the best possible start to learning English.

Intermediate for Children

This level will focus on building vocabulary, grammar and basic phonic skills using resources appropriate for younger learners. The classes are engaging, interactive and will get learners excited about speaking English. Students progress will be monitored and assessments will be given every three months. This course is based on the world recognized Cambridge curriculum so you can be confident in the curriculum and methodology used.
