About Us


We are an online educational platform created for students from all over the world to learn English with personalized courses and lessons. Easy English Canada was established in 2024 with hopes of bringing convenience and comfort to our students. People are increasingly busy in today's competitive world so we hope to simplify language learning without taking shortcuts on quality. We offer lessons for ages as young as eight years old up to adults at all levels ranging from beginner (A1) to advanced (C2). Our team are all native speaking, qualified ESL teachers with at least 2 or more years of teaching experience with foreign students.

  • Courses


    - Our courses are categorized into 4 levels: beginner, intermediate, IELTS prep, and advanced conversational. Lesson planning can be flexible and customized to suit the learner's needs. We use well-recognized curriculums as well as the latest teaching methodology to give our students the easiest and most enjoyable language learning experience. All levels will include periodic assessments and will give learners the chance to receive personalized feedback. Contact us to learn more about our curriculums and learning paths.
