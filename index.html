About Us


We are a Canada based online educational platform created for students from all over the world to learn English with personalized courses and lessons. Easy English Canada was established in 2024 with hopes of bringing convenience to our students and expanding language learning access worldwide. People are increasingly busy in today's competitive world so we hope to simplify language learning without taking shortcuts on quality. We offer lessons for ages as young as eight years old up to adults at all levels ranging from beginner (A1) to advanced (C2). Our team are all native speaking, qualified ESL teachers with at least 2 or more years of teaching experience with foreign students. Classes can vary in size from one to six students. One on one lessons are available upon request but for most levels it's recommended to study with others to optimize interactions in speaking activities.
