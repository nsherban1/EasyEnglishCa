Beginner Adult Pathway

Practice speaking with a qualified teacher and other students about complex topics such as technology, politics, education, history and more. Learn how to debate a wide range of topics and defend your viewpoints. Receive personalized feedback related to grammar use and pronunciation. The hours are flexible so students with busy schedules can still find time to study. This level is recommended for ages 16 and higher and students should be at least upper-intermediate in English.
